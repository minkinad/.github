# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want these projects to be a respectful, welcoming place for contributors of all experience levels and backgrounds.

Participation in the project should be harassment-free for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

Examples of behavior that help the community:

- Use clear, respectful, and welcoming language.
- Assume good intent, but stay honest about problems and tradeoffs.
- Accept feedback gracefully and focus on improving the work.
- Keep discussions constructive and relevant to the project.
- Show empathy toward other contributors and maintainers.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or deliberately inflammatory comments.
- Sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Publishing someone else's private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional or collaborative setting.

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for setting expectations, moderating discussions, and taking fair action in response to behavior that harms the project or its contributors.

They may edit, remove, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that are not aligned with this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately through the contact methods listed on the maintainer profile or website:

- https://github.com/minkinad
- https://minkin.tech

Reports will be reviewed and handled as carefully and confidentially as possible.
